Relax. is a tiny, quiet space on the internet.

This app was built as a reminder:
You don't have to be productive right now.

The world constantly demands attention. But this?
This is a little corner that asks nothing of you.

Why Relax?

Relax isn't about being chill in an Instagram way. It's about stopping.
Stopping the need to optimize, to impress, to perform.
Even for just a minute.

You might call it a minimalist alarm clock. Or a meditation timer.
But honestly, it's just a soft tool to help you sit down and be.

A note on simplicity.

People say things are brutally simple. But maybe it's more like peacefully simple.
This app isn't trying to be clever. It's not trying to keep you engaged.

It's just a quiet page with a timer, a sound, and a place to relax.

And if all this page does is remind you to sit, breathe, and not do, even for a moment,
then that's more than enough.
The rest of the world can wait.

If this space helps you breathe a little easier, I've done my job.
Thanks for being here. Come back any time.
